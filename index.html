00 — About

What is this?

/dev/null is the Unix black hole — anything written to it disappears without a trace. That's a reasonable description of most personal websites too.

This one is being built in the open, mostly as a learning exercise. The design system is intentional; the content is still catching up. Check back when the nav links stop being grayed out.

shell
ls -la /dev/null
crw-rw-rw-  1 root  wheel  3, 2  devnull.tech

echo "hello world" > /dev/null
# output: none. that's the point.
